Privacy Policy — ScreenRec

Effective date: 2026-06-07
Contact: qotsaman@gmail.com

Who We Are

ScreenRec is a simple Android app, created by an independent developer, that records the device's screen together with audio into a video file.

What Data We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app works completely offline. It has no account, no sign-in, and no internet connection. We operate no servers that receive any data from your device.

Your Recordings Stay on the Device

When you record, the resulting video (screen image plus system and optional microphone audio) is saved only on your own device, in the public Movies/ScreenRec folder, where it appears in your Gallery. Recordings are never uploaded, never sent to the developer, and never shared with any third party, analytics service, or advertising network. The developer cannot see or access your recordings.

Permissions We Use

  • Screen capture (Android's MediaProjection consent): asked every time you start a recording, so the app can capture the screen image.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): used to capture audio for the recording — both the device's playback audio and, if you leave the microphone switch on, your microphone. Audio is written only into the local video file.
  • Notifications: to show an ongoing notification while recording, with a Stop button.

No audio or video is processed off the device.

No Advertising, No Analytics

The app contains no advertisements, no advertising SDKs, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no tracking of any kind.

Deleting Your Data

Because nothing leaves the device, there is nothing for us to delete on our end. Your recordings are ordinary video files — delete them from the Gallery or file manager at any time. Uninstalling the app does not delete recordings you have already saved to the Gallery.

Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated with a new effective date.
